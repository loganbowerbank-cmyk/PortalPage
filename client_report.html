General Experience and Reflections

What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?

Learning how to use GitHub. I was able to by the grace of Google Gemini be able to learn how to navigate the site and publish, add files, and share with others.

What did you enjoy most about this project?

Bringing something to life. I wouldn't have thought of any the other ideas so being able to then create that idea from nothing was very satisfying and made me proud of whta I had accomplished.

What would you do differently if you could do the project again?

I would try and make the site look even better. Right now it is great but it is nothing compared to sites for large companies that have dynamic movement of pages and other eyecandy that I just wasn't able to implement.

Do you feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience? Explain.

Yes. Now that I have experience with GitHub and working with other people on projects, I think that I would have a much easier time on a real-world project for another client.
